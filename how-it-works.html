Here’s how you can get instant access to high-quality, ready-made lessons:

1. Choose Your Class

Explore our growing collection of thoughtfully designed, ready-to-use lessons. All lessons are built on Miro, ensuring a smooth, interactive setup. Simply browse through the options and select the one that best fits your lesson plan or teaching goals.

2. Download & Connect

Click the Download button on your chosen lesson. You’ll be instantly redirected to our Telegram Sales Assistant—no account needed. Just send a quick message to get started.

3. Place Your Order

Let our assistant know which class you’d like to purchase. They’ll walk you through the quick and easy ordering process, answering any questions along the way.

4. Get Instant Access

Once your payment is confirmed, you'll receive an instant access link to your selected lesson on Miro.

5. Teach Without the Prep Hassle

Jump right into teaching! Each class is fully interactive, beautifully structured on Miro, and includes a Quizlet set to help your students review and reinforce what they’ve learned. Save time on prep and focus on what trully matters!
